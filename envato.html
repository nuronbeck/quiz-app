Luma Vue - Nuxt.js & Vue.js Learning Management System Admin Template

Vue.js Learning Management System

Luma Vue is a beautifully crafted user interface for modern Education Platforms built in Vue.js and Nuxt. It includes Courses & Tutorials, Video Lessons, Student and Teacher Dashboard, Curriculum Management, Earnings and Reporting, ERP, HR, CMS, Tasks, Projects, eCommerce and more.
